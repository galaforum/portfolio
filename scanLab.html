ScanLab

Role UX Designer
Team Size and Duration 1 member, 1 month
Tools Adobe XD

Background

The idea of ScanLab was a product of the rapid prototyping class I took one semester. Students were required to create sketches and submit them in a document format (PDF format). This requirement led to using two devices (mobile phone and laptop) and two to three applications (scanning application on the phone, an application like Gmail or Drive to transfer the image to your laptop and a word document). The process became tedious resulting in my decision to reduce the number of devices and applications to one. I also wanted the additional features of auto-detection of the scan area in the image, easy collaboration with registered users and removal of watermarks. ScanLab will help achieve this goal.

Research and initial sketching

There are image scanning applications in the android AppStore, however, none provide all the features I aspire for. Most applications add digital watermarks reducing social sharing and a negative image for the user. None covered provide collaboration with other users to let them add and edit images in the same document. After the research phase, I did some prototyping as an initial step to digital prototyping.

Next steps

The application prototype received very positive feedback and in the next iteration, I will enhance the interface to improve user experience and add the functionality of giving different access levels (view only, view and edit access) to different collaborators.
